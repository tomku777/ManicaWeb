Speakers

Jan Vejmelek
Chief Economist, KB
Jan Vejmělek is Chief Economist of Komerční banka, an affiliated member of the SG Group, since December 2010. He joined Komerční banka in 1998 as a securities market analyst. Since 2002, he has been Head of Economic and Strategic Research in Komerční banka’s Investment Banking division. Jan primarily focuses on macroeconomic analysis of the Czech economy and on the forex market. Jan Vejmělek graduated from the Faculty of Finance and Accounting of the University of Economics in Prague (with a major in Finance as well as Economics of Industrial Enterprises). In 2003, he completed a doctoral program in Economic Policy and Management, specializing in Finance, at the same faculty. Between 2004 and 2006, Jan completed a postgraduate program on a distance learning basis at CFA Institute.
Ondřej Michalák
Software Program Director for Central and Eastern Europe
Ondrej Michalak is software program director for IDC in Central and Eastern Europe. His responsibilities cover traditional enterprise applications, as well as virtual and augmented reality, data analytics, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and cognitive systems. Ondrej is especially focused on use cases of new technologies and how they modernize, innovate, and disrupt traditional business. He is certified for a big data and data science solutions, and once founded a startup focused on using virtual reality for data visualization and analytics. His key strength is the ability to cover both the technical and business aspects of the software industry through his combination of hands-on technical experience and years spent in business development and sales strategy.
