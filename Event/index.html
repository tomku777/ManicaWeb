Speakers

Jan Vejmelek (panel moderator)
Chief Economist, KB
Jan Vejmělek is Chief Economist of Komerční banka, an affiliated member of the SG Group, since December 2010. He joined Komerční banka in 1998 as a securities market analyst. Since 2002, he has been Head of Economic and Strategic Research in Komerční banka’s Investment Banking division. Jan primarily focuses on macroeconomic analysis of the Czech economy and on the forex market. Jan Vejmělek graduated from the Faculty of Finance and Accounting of the University of Economics in Prague (with a major in Finance as well as Economics of Industrial Enterprises). In 2003, he completed a doctoral program in Economic Policy and Management, specializing in Finance, at the same faculty. Between 2004 and 2006, Jan completed a postgraduate program on a distance learning basis at CFA Institute.
Ondřej Michalák (speaker)
Software Program Director for Central and Eastern Europe, IDC
Ondrej Michalak is software program director for IDC in Central and Eastern Europe. His responsibilities cover traditional enterprise applications, as well as virtual and augmented reality, data analytics, machine learning, artificial intelligence, and cognitive systems. Ondrej is especially focused on use cases of new technologies and how they modernize, innovate, and disrupt traditional business. He is certified for a big data and data science solutions, and once founded a startup focused on using virtual reality for data visualization and analytics. His key strength is the ability to cover both the technical and business aspects of the software industry through his combination of hands-on technical experience and years spent in business development and sales strategy.
Filip Doušek (speaker)
CEO, Stories
Filip has been been specialising on company management via big data for the past 17 years. He also wrote a novel Hejno bez ptáků (Flock without birds). Nowadays he is a CEO of Stories startup, which focuses on radical companies performance improvement. Filip used to work as a SAP consultant in the Czech Republic, UK, Malaisia and Hong Kong, among his clients being brands such as Burberry, Clarks, Baťa or Wrigley.
Tomáš Lancinger (speaker)
Data Science Manager, KB
Tomas Lancinger is innovation seeker who founded and leads Data Science in Komerční banka. He has technical background and 15+ years of experience in IT and information management. Majority of his career is bound with KB and SG. Tomas is currently focused on getting business value from Big Data through AI and delivers various data products despite full corporate support.

Mark Yates (speaker)
Research Manager, IDC Insights, Central & Eastern Europe, IDC
Mark Yates brings more than 20 years' experience to his role as a digital transformation analyst. Working closely with other analysts and consultants, he helps translate the tenets of digital transformation into practical strategies and actions. He identifies emerging business models that can upend cash cows and generate new revenue streams. He helps firms identify pain points and the primary obstacles preventing more rapid transformation. And he assesses market dynamics to determine which technologies will be hot and where IT suppliers and enterprise users should invest their time and talent.

Prior to taking on the role of analyst, Mr. Yates worked in marketing, communications, and training for companies in CEE and the UK. He served as IDC CEE’s lead analysts for Government Insights, as IDC EMEA’s interim marketing director, and as IDC CEMA’s marketing manager. In addition to promoting IDC products and services, he helped analysts turn complex research into accessible summaries, briefs, and press releases. He also gave in-depth presentations on IT markets at IDC events across the region. Outside IDC, Mr. Yates has worked with and for telcos, IT security firms, training agencies, and a startup.

Sylvie Clemot (speaker)
Head of GTS EUS, Société Générale
For almost 25 years in the Banking industry, all her roles have been related to IT Infrastructure. Sylvie joined Societe Generale in 1993 as a graduate of a tier 1 French Engineering School. After the first 10 years in the telecom, network and telephone teams, both for the Corporate and for the Investment Bank, she took quickly on management roles. Then she spent the next decade in COO and Financial responsibilities for the IT Infrastructure; being, from its creation in 2009 to end of 2012, the Financial director of the SG Group IT Infrastructure Direction. Since 2013, Sylvie Clemot leads the Digital Workplace Services of the Group on a global scale, encompassing from innovation and architecture to user support on all digital workplace solutions.
Mark Child (speaker)
.x.x.x.x.x.x., IDC
With more than a decade of experience researching software and security markets in the region, Mark Child is IDC’s Research Manager for Software and Enterprise Applications in Central and Eastern Europe and is also the CEE Security Practice Lead. Within the software group, Child manages analyst teams and coordinates research and projects, producing in-depth software market studies, trackers, and custom consulting covering the Czech and Slovak republics and the CEE region. His security role adds further dimensions of focused end-user research and agenda creation for one of IDC's most successful event series, the CEE Security Roadshow. Child is a regular keynote speaker at IDC events, particularly in the security area, and also supports IDC's global initiatives, such as the security spending guide.
Petr Chaloupka (speaker)
CEO, GreyCortex
Petr Chaloupka is a CEO of GreyCortex, producer of advanced tool GreyCortex Mendel, designed to combat cybercrime. As a CEO, he leads teams specializing in advanced AI, machine learning and Big Data analysis within the company. Petr has a lengthy background in security business, where he has spent several years in management roles of AVG Technologies (being Avast nowadays). Prior to that, he had worked as CEO VAD of Comguard, or as a CIO in Konica Minolta.
Stephane Nappo (speaker)
Global Head Information Security, Société Générale
Stephane Nappo is the Global Head Information Security for Société Générale International Banking & Financial Services pole since 2011. Present in 67 countries, this pole employs over 67, 000 people and has 30 millions clients distributed within 40 autonomous banks and 90 entities all delivering financial services.

He was senior consultant specializing in IT security as of 1995. His extensive training in telecom, business administration and law, allows him to have a unique approach towards solving technological and business related issues. He worked for over 80 organizations in numerous sectors.

He implements conventional risk management methods with a systemic approach to complex problems. Based in Paris, he operates regularly in Russia, Central Europe and Africa. His current mission targets digital services security, anti-fraud prevention, incident response and information security’s digital transformation.
